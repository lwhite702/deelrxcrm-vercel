# Customer Management

Comprehensive customer relationship management with detailed profiles, interaction history, and automated workflows.

## Overview

The DeelRx CRM customer management system provides a centralized hub for all customer interactions, from initial contact through ongoing relationship management.

## Key Features

### Customer Profiles
- **Complete Information**: Name, contact details, preferences, and custom fields
- **Interaction History**: Complete timeline of all customer touchpoints
- **Segmentation**: Automatic and manual customer categorization
- **Custom Fields**: Flexible data capture for industry-specific needs

### Contact Management
- **Multiple Contacts**: Support for multiple contacts per customer
- **Communication Preferences**: Email, phone, SMS preferences per contact
- **Role Tracking**: Decision makers, influencers, and other stakeholders

### Activity Tracking
- **Interaction Logging**: Automatic capture of emails, calls, and meetings
- **Task Management**: Follow-up reminders and scheduled activities
- **Notes & Comments**: Team collaboration on customer insights

## Customer Operations

### Creating Customers

```javascript
// Create a new customer
const customer = await crm.customers.create({
  name: "Acme Corporation",
  email: "contact@acme.com",
  phone: "+1-555-0123",
  address: {
    street: "123 Business Ave",
    city: "Enterprise City",
    state: "CA",
    zip: "90210"
  },
  customFields: {
    industry: "Technology",
    employees: "50-100"
  }
});
```
