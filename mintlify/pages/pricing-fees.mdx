## Payment Processing Fees

### Manual Payments - **Available Now**

<Callout type="success">
**No Platform Fees for Manual Reconciliation**

All manual payment methods are processed completely free with no platform fees:
- Zelle, Apple Pay, Cash App, Venmo, PayPal
- Cash, Check, Money Order, Bank Transfer
- Any custom payment method
</Callout>

**Supported Methods:**
- **Electronic**: Zelle, Apple Pay, Cash App, Venmo, PayPal, Bank Transfer
- **Physical**: Cash, Check, Money Order
- **Custom**: Any other payment method you accept

**Benefits:**
- Zero platform fees
- Instant payment recording
- Full transaction history
- Automated reconciliation tools

### Card Processing - <Badge variant="outline">Coming Soon</Badge>

When card processing becomes available, all fees will be transparently disclosed:

| Fee Component | Rate | Description |
|---------------|------|-------------|
| **Base Processing Fee** | 3.5% + $0.30 | Standard card processing |
| **Street Tax** | +0.5% | Additional processing overhead |
| **Instant Payout** | +1.0% | Same-day fund availability |
| **Total Maximum Fee** | **5.0% + $0.30** | All-inclusive fee structure |

**Example Calculations:**
- $100 transaction: $5.30 maximum fee (5.3%)
- $50 transaction: $2.80 maximum fee (5.6%)
- $25 transaction: $1.55 maximum fee (6.2%)

Unlike competitors who hide fees in "processing costs" or monthly charges, we provide complete transparency:
- No hidden fees or surprise charges
- All fees disclosed upfront
- Simple, predictable pricing structure
- No long-term contracts required
</Callout>
